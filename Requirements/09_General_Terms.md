# General Terms & Conditions (Development-Relevant)

**Source:** Requirements.pdf pages 17-19, 23-27

## Intellectual Property

- All deliverables (source code, models, documentation, training data) become **exclusive property of the Ministry of Culture**
- Vendor retains no rights to the developed system
- All third-party open-source components must have **compatible licenses** (MIT, Apache 2.0, etc.)
- License inventory must be documented

## Warranty Period

- **12-month warranty** from date of Go-Live
- Covers all bug fixes, security patches, and performance issues
- No additional cost during warranty period
- Warranty runs concurrently with Year 1 of AMC

## Payment Terms

Payment is milestone-based:

| Milestone | Payment % |
|---|---|
| Contract signing + Project kickoff | 10% |
| PoC delivery and acceptance | 20% |
| Full system deployment | 30% |
| UAT sign-off and Go-Live | 25% |
| Documentation delivery | 15% |

## Penalties

### Delay Penalties
- **0.5% per week** of delay beyond scheduled milestone dates
- Maximum penalty cap: 10% of total contract value
- Force majeure exclusions apply

### Performance Penalties
- If system fails to meet 99.5% uptime SLA → proportional penalty
- If response time consistently exceeds 5s p95 → remediation required within defined timeframe

## Change Management

- Changes to scope must be formally requested and approved by both parties
- Impact assessment required for all change requests
- Changes to Requirements.pdf require formal amendment (document is **FROZEN**)
- Design document can be updated with mutual agreement

## Confidentiality

- All Ministry data is confidential
- NDA required
- No data sharing with third parties
- Data must not leave Indian territory
- Vendor staff must undergo security clearance if required

## Team Requirements

- Dedicated project manager
- Technical architect
- AI/ML specialists with Hindi NLP experience
- Frontend developers
- DevOps/Infrastructure engineers
- QA/Testing specialists
- Minimum team composition specified in eligibility criteria

## Knowledge Transfer

- Complete knowledge transfer to Ministry IT staff
- Training sessions (minimum 3 batches):
  1. Admin training — system management, configuration, monitoring
  2. End-user training — chatbot and search usage
  3. Technical training — architecture, deployment, troubleshooting
- Training materials as deliverables
- Handover documentation
