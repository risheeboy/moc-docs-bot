## Welcome!

The Ministry of Culture website now features an intelligent AI-powered assistant that can answer your questions about Indian culture, heritage, monuments, arts, and government programs.

This guide will help you use two main features:
1. **Conversational Chatbot** — Ask questions and get instant answers
2. **Semantic Search** — Find relevant documents and information across all Ministry websites

---

## Feature 1: Conversational Chatbot

### How to Access

1. Visit **https://culture.gov.in**
2. Look for the **floating chat bubble** in the bottom-right corner
3. Click to open the chat widget

### How to Use

#### Starting a Conversation

1. Type your question in the text input box
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send button**
3. Wait for the AI to process and respond (usually 2-5 seconds)
4. The AI will provide an answer with source citations

#### Example Questions

```
"What are the major heritage sites in India?"
"Tell me about the Taj Mahal"
"How do I apply for cultural scholarships?"
"What are the timings of the National Museum?"
```

### Multi-turn Conversation

The chatbot remembers previous messages in your conversation:

1. **First turn:** "What is the Ayodhya Ram Mandir?"
   - AI responds with information
2. **Second turn:** "When was it constructed?"
   - AI understands context and refers back to the first question
3. **Third turn:** "Can I visit it?"
   - AI continues the conversation about the same topic

**Note:** Conversations are kept private and deleted after 90 days automatically.

### Understanding the Response

Each response includes:

| Component | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Response Text** | The AI's answer to your question |
| **Sources** | Links to the original documents used. Click to read full content |
| **Confidence** | How confident the AI is (shown as percentage). Higher = more reliable |
| **Language** | Language of the response (English or Hindi) |

### Using Voice

**Ask a question by voice:**

1. Click the **microphone icon** at the bottom of the chat
2. Speak your question clearly
3. AI transcribes and responds automatically

**Listen to the response:**

1. Click the **speaker icon** next to the response
2. AI speaks the answer aloud in your chosen language
3. Adjust volume as needed

### Language Toggle

1. Use the **English/Hindi button** at the top of the chat
2. All subsequent messages will be in your chosen language
3. Previous messages stay in their original language

### Providing Feedback

Help improve the AI:

1. After reading the response, click the **rating button** (1-5 stars)
2. Optionally, add a comment explaining your feedback
3. Your feedback helps the system improve

### Clearing Your Conversation

1. Click the **menu icon** (three dots)
2. Select **"Clear conversation"**
3. All messages will be deleted permanently

## Feature 2: Semantic Search

### How to Access

1. Visit **https://culture.gov.in**
2. Click the **Search button** in the main navigation
3. Or go directly to **https://culture.gov.in/search**

### How to Use Search

#### Basic Search

1. Type your query in the search box (e.g., "heritage monuments")
2. Press **Enter** or click **Search**
3. Review results on the page

#### Advanced Filtering

After searching, use filters to narrow results:

**By Source:**
- Ministry of Culture
- Archaeological Survey of India
- National Museum
- UNESCO Sites
- (and others)

**By Content Type:**
- Webpages
- PDFs
- Images
- Videos

**By Date:**
- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Last year
- Any time

**By Language:**
- English
- Hindi
- Both

### Understanding Search Results

Each result shows:

| Item | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Document headline |
| **Summary** | AI-generated brief overview (not exact text) |
| **Source** | Which website this came from |
| **Date** | When it was published |
| **Thumbnail** | Image preview if available |

### Sorting Results

Click the **Sort** dropdown to change order:

- **Relevance** (default): Most matching your query
- **Newest First**: Latest published first
- **Oldest First**: Earliest first
- **Popularity**: Most viewed

### Pagination

- Results shown **20 per page**
- Use **Previous/Next buttons** to navigate
- Or jump to a specific page with the **page selector**

### Viewing Full Content

1. Click on a search result
2. Browser opens the original page on the source website
3. Read the full content there

### Image & Video Results

Below text results, you'll see **images and videos**:

1. Click any image/video thumbnail
2. Opens in a larger view
3. Shows the source website

### Events Section

Search results include **upcoming Ministry events**:

- Exhibition dates
- Cultural programs
- Workshop registrations
- Festival information

## Common Questions

### Q: What if the AI gives incorrect information?

**A:**
1. Click the thumbs-down icon under the response
2. Leave feedback explaining the error
3. Your feedback helps improve the system
4. Always verify important information using the source links

### Q: How do I report a bug?

**A:**
Click the **Report Issue** button in the app and describe:
- What you were trying to do
- What went wrong
- What you expected to happen

### Q: Can I download search results?

**A:**
Yes! Click the **Download** button on search results page to get:
- PDF of all results with sources
- CSV spreadsheet for further analysis

### Q: Does the chatbot know my personal information?

**A:**
No. The chatbot:
- Cannot access your account
- Doesn't know your name or email
- Doesn't store anything between sessions
- Forgets your conversation after 90 days

### Q: What languages are supported?

**A:**
Currently supported:
- **English** — Full support
- **Hindi** — Full support

More languages coming soon!

### Q: Can I use this offline?

**A:**
No, currently requires internet connection to:
- Query the AI
- Search documents
- Use speech features

### Q: Who can see my conversation?

**A:**
Only you can see your conversation:
- Stored securely on government servers
- Never shared with external parties
- Automatically deleted after 90 days
- You can manually delete anytime

## Tips & Tricks

### Search More Effectively

- Be **specific:** "Taj Mahal restoration" instead of "monuments"
- Use **multiple words:** Search captures context
- Try **different phrasings:** If first search doesn't help
- **Check filters:** Narrow by date or source

### Get Better Chat Answers

- Ask **specific questions** rather than broad topics
- Provide **context** if continuing a conversation
- Ask **follow-up questions** naturally
- Use language you're comfortable with

### Save Information

1. Click the **bookmark icon** next to a search result
2. Results saved to your browser bookmarks
3. Create a reading list for later
